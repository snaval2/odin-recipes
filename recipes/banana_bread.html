Best Ever Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, softened
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and butter. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the mashed bananas, milk, and vanilla extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the banana mixture, stirring just until combined.
  4. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  5. Let the bread cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
